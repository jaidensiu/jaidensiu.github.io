## Preface

Out of the 5 co-op/internship positions I did, I focused heavily on some aspect of Android development and wrote a lot
of Kotlin for each job. Starting university/college, I had planned to explore a whole bunch of different stacks and
domains ranging from backend engineering to data science, which was often recommended by more-senior peers and industry
professionals. Initially, I was worried about pigeonholing myself and limiting my growth although my perspective has
drastically changed over time. In this blog post, I hope to share my experience and insights of specializing early in
software engineering.
